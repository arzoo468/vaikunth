About

Vaikunth is another name for heaven or paradise, designed by our Supreme Father Himself for his beloved children

Know ourself & our Real Father

We are all souls or energies wearing the physical costume called "body". Without the body we resemble a tiny spark of light.
Our real Father is also a soul or energy. He too resembles a tiny spark of light. However He does not wear any physical costume called "body". He is not born, nor does He ever die. He is the Highest of High-the Supreme soul.
  • Appearance: A tiny point of light
  • Name: Shiv-the benefactor
  • Address: The 6th Element-The abode of souls
  • Occupation: Purification & Creating Heaven
We address God as Father, as He is the Father of all souls. He comes only once to this world of 5 elements, after every 5000 years. He teaches Rajyoga.

RAJYOGA

Rajyoga is the Art & Science of communion with the Supreme energy. With this technique, the souls get cleansed, rejuvenated and the divine qualities start emerging. It is then that the transformation takes place-from human beings to deities, from hell to heaven. This very earth which is now hell, transforms into Heaven.
